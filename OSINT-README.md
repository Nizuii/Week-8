# üåê What is OSINT?
<ul>
  <li><strong>OSINT</strong> or Open Source Intelligence, is the practice of collecting information from publicly available sources (like websites, news article, social media, etc...) and using it to gain useful knowledge or answer specific questions.</li>
</ul>

## How OSINT Works?
Anyone can use <strong>OSINT</strong> by searching for information online, such as finding details aout a person, company, or a topic using search engines, social media or other open databases. The process involve defining what you want to find, collecting data, checking if its reliable, analyzing for patterns, and then reporting or using your finding.

## Why OSINT is important?
<strong>OSINT</strong> is valuable in areas such as cyber security, digital investigations, business and journalism because it helps people and organizations make informed decision based on information that is already available to everyone.

## OSINT Real world applications.
OSINT has many real world applications across various fields, especially in cyber security and beyond.
### 1. Cyber Security.
<ul>
  <li>Identifying cyber threats and threat actors by monitoring hackers forums, social media and dark web sources.</li>
  <li>Detecting data breaches and leaked credentials to prevent attacks.</li>
  <li>Conducting risk assessments of companies by analyzin their online exposure.</li>
  <li>Supporting incident response and digital forensics investigation with publicly available intelligence.</li>
</ul>
