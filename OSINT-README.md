# What is OSINT?
OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) means gathering information about the target whether its a person's or a company's information that is publicly available. The information can be a print or electronic form. This publicly available information is generally divided into 6 categories:
<ol>
  <li><strong>Public media:</strong> Includes newspaper, magazines, telivisions, radio braodcasts..</li>
  <li><strong>Internet:</strong> Includes informations from blogs, websites, forms, social media sites.</li>
  <li><strong>Public government data:</strong>Includes Reports, Budgets, Press conference, hearings and speeches.</li>
  <li><strong>Professional and academic publications:</strong> Includes journals, conferences, academic papers.</li>
